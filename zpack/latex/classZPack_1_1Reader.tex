\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Archive reader. 

Usage example\+: 
\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{\textcolor{comment}{// Open the archive}}
\DoxyCodeLine{\mbox{\hyperlink{classZPack_1_1Reader}{ZPack::Reader}} reader(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}archive.zpk"{}});}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{\textcolor{comment}{// Unpack files}}
\DoxyCodeLine{std::ifstream file(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}file.txt"{}});}
\DoxyCodeLine{reader.unpackFile(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}path/to/file.txt"{}}, file);}
\DoxyCodeLine{file.close();}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{\textcolor{comment}{// Streaming API}}
\DoxyCodeLine{std::ifstream file2(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}file2.txt"{}});}
\DoxyCodeLine{reader.unpackFileStream(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}path/to/file2.txt"{}}, file2);}
\DoxyCodeLine{file2.close();}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{\textcolor{comment}{// Close the file}}
\DoxyCodeLine{reader.closeFile();}

\end{DoxyCode}
 The reader can be reused as many times as you want.
